Barbecue Grilled Chicken

About Me

This is a super simple and amazing recepie! I make it all of the time. Any grill will do, gas or charcoal. If you don't have a grill, get one. If you don't want to buy one I guess you can pan sear the chicken on your stovetop.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs (Thighs have more fat and flavor than breasts)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Your favorite barbecue sauce
  1. Salt and pepper both sides of each piece of chicken.
  2. Baste the babercue sauce on each piece of chicken.
  3. Heat the grill to between 375 and 400 degrees.
  4. Grill the chicken, flipping every 3 to 4 minutes. Make sure to baste on more sauce every time you flip.
